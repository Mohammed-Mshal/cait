Learn More About the central agency for information technology

CAIT is the home of expertise and reference in the field of information technology in the government sector, to contribute to the process of digital transformation and the gradual transition of Kuwaiti society towards modernity and sustainable future development in which information technology plays a fundamental and effective role in comprehensive development, and upgrading the work system to achieve the concept of "e-government" by linking government agencies systems through a unified information network that allows citizens to complete transactions electronically through a "government electronic" portal...
